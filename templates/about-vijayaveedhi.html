About

VIJAYAVEEDHI

Vijayaveedhi is a popular project in Kerala which is part of the State Government's 100 Day Action Plan and is being implemented at the Panchayat level as per Government of Kerala Order No. G.O. (Rt) No.1103/2020/ID, Dated Thiruvananthapuram 24-12-2020 formulated by the Department of Commerce and Industry.

Unemployed youngsters and job seekers are longing for permanent jobs in government departments, in governmental agencies or in public sector. Recruitments to these institutions are based on competitive examinations. Hence the educated job seekers are looking for credible coaching centres to get trained for appearing the competitive examinations. Kerala State Rutronix being a reputed government-controlled training institution can effectively involve in the training programme utilising its rich experience ... in conducting various computer and IT based programmes throughout the state. Rutronix will able to provide the training programme whole over the state in a uniform and structured manner affordable to common public. This may give an opportunity to all unemployed youth to compete in the competitive examinations. In this context, a training programme, named- “Vijayaveedhi” has been designed by Rutronix for imparting training to the employment seekers for attending various competitive examinations for the recruitments conducted by KPSC, UPSC, Staff Selection Commission, Banking Service Recruitment Boards, Railway Recruitment Board, various Governmental and Public sector agencies, bodies etc.
