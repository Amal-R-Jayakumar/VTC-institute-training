Vijayaveedhi Training Center

Vijayaveedhi is a popular project in Kerala which is part of the State Government's 100 Day Action Plan and is being implemented at the Panchayat level as per Government of Kerala Order No. G.O. (Rt) No.1103/2020/ID, Dated Thiruvananthapuram 24-12-2020 formulated by the Department of Commerce and Industry. Training to select candidates for vacancies in State / Central Government Institutions to enable them to appear for competitive examinations conducted by PSC, UPSC, Banking Service Recruitment Board, Railway Recruitment Board, Staff Selection Commission etc. The project aims to cater to everyone, both urban and rural, at minimal cost.

Vijayaveedhi Training Centers will be sanctioned in one panchayat, two in municipalities and three in corporations. Individuals with existing institutions and new entrepreneurs can apply for Vijayavithi Learning Centers. Applications can be submitted to colleges, schools, parallel colleges, Akshaya e-centers, women's co-operative societies, trust-owned institutions, partnerships, other individual institutions and new entrepreneurs. Permission for VTC can be obtained on the basis of scrutiny of applications and direct scrutiny by the concerned officials. Priority will be given in allocating study centers to women, transgender people, persons with disabilities and expatriates who have lost their jobs due to the covid crisis.
